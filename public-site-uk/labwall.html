H5P Workshops

All labs's in Module

    Moodle-Basics-Lab-01

    In this lab we will look at putting the basics of your Moodle module in place, such hiding sections that are not in use and uploading a module image.

    Moodle-Basics-Lab-02

    The following workshop is practical by nature, it uses a series of steps to guide you through tasks that will help you developer an understanding of how to integrate Bootstrap into your modules in Moodle.

    Drag-&-Drop-Lab-01

    You will create H5P Drag and Drop component that will use questions that uses both text and images as drag-able alternatives and you will learn how this integrates into a Moodle module.

    Flashcards-Lab-01

    In this workshop you will create a Flashcard that's intuitive in relation to content you may have covered in class, the flashcard will have images that the user has to pair with questions and answers in the activity.

    Presentation-Lab-01

    In this lab you will create an Interactive Presentation that will include some multiple choice questions and you will also learn how to to integrate this H5P component into Moodle.

    Timeline-Lab-01

    In this lab you will create an Interactive Timeline, this may be of any particular topic, in this example I have chosen to use the timeline of the Internet, this activity will show you how to create this timeline with the use of images and text and you will also learn how to to integrate this H5P component into Moodle.

    Video-Lab-01

    In this lab you will create an Interactive Video, this may be of any particular topic, in this example I have chosen to use a video of people walking on a footpath, this activity will show you how to create this video, include multiple choice questions and you will also learn how to to integrate this H5P component into Moodle.
